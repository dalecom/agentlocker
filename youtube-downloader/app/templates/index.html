How to use the YouTube MP3 & MP4 Downloader

  1. Enter the YouTube URL: Paste the URL of the YouTube video you want to download into the input field labeled "YouTube URL."
  2. Select the Format: Choose between MP3 (audio only) or MP4 (video with audio). MP4 options will display additional quality settings.
  3. Choose Video Quality (for MP4): If you selected MP4, pick the desired video quality from the dropdown menu. Options include Best, 720p, 1080p, and 4K.
  4. Click Download: Press the "Download" button to start the download process. A spinner will indicate that the download is in progress.
  5. Download Your File: Once the download is ready, the button will become active. Click the "Download File" link to save the file to your device.
